   Twitter Search makes it easy for users to search tweets by date.
   These columns mean as follows.

  • Username: Searching twitter username (Example: @plus_place)
  • Date: Searching date (Example: 2011-10-22)

   Click date column to show a calendar. And click date cell you want to search. Click '<<' or '>>' to show previous or next month on the calendar. Click 'Done' to close the calendar.

   Twitter Search can search tweets whether you append '@' to top of username or not.
